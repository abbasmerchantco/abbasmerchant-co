A framework is what's left after someone compresses a decade of experience into a diagram. Useful — but the compression is lossy. What got thrown away is exactly the part you needed: the ten thousand small judgment calls about *when it applies*.

Reading the framework gives you the map. Only reps give you the territory. I keep having to relearn this, one confident misapplication at a time.
